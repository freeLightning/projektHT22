Healing Mantras

Released January 2013

Musicians:

  • Deva Premal - Vocals
  • Gyuto Monks Sonam Dorjee, Passang Gyamtso, Gelek Gonpo and Tenzin Jigme - Vocals
  • David Darling - Cello
  • Kit Walker - Keyboards

Peace. Abundance. Compassion. Physical healing. These are just some of the universal aspirations we can usher into our lives through dedicated mantra practice. At once a serene listening experience and a powerful tool for transformation, Deva Premal's Healing Mantras presents 17 Hindu and Tibetan Buddhist chants, each repeated 108 times in keeping with sacred tradition.
Note: Deva Premal's Healing Mantras Combines two previously released titles:

TIMELESS

Released September 2022

Musicians:

  • Deva Premal, Vocals
  • Miten, Guitar & Vocals
  • India Arie, Vocals
  • Jai Uttal, Instrumentalist
  • Joby Baker, Producer/multi-instrumentalist & Vocals

TIMELESS is the new album from the beloved, mantra music duo, Deva Premal and Miten. It is an album of inspiration, healing and hope for our troubled times. Soothing mantras, glimmering, gemlike musical soundscapes and the sublime voice of Deva Premal and her soulmate Miten take the listener to a blissful place of calm and serenity. Grammy-winning singer-songwriter India Arie lends her own incandescent voice to the album’s key track “Tara Mangalartha Mantra,” a prayer for climate balance.

The sole English lyric on TIMELESS, I Just Close My Eyes, is a breezy, bossa groove underpins a hushed, heartfelt vocal performance by Miten. This song dates from Miten’s time, living in the ashram of the Indian mystic, Osho.
